Explore Our Programs

Children's Jiu-Jitsu

Our Jiu-Jitsu programs are great for kids. With a focus on traditional jiu-jitsu, they will learn and practice a wide range of techniques including strikes, throws, joint locks, grappling, and submissions. Our jiu-jitsu classes offer many benefits:

  • Improve cardiovascular health
  • Increase strength and endurance
  • Increase flexibility, agility, and coordination
  • Develop falling skills
  • Increase confidence and self-esteem
  • Develop patience and discipline
  • Increase proprioception
  • Increase vO2 max

Our Instructors

Meet Our Instructors

The Iceman - Jean-Yves Thériault


Jean-Yves is the lead instructor for the kickboxing program. Undefeated in the 80s and a 23 time world champion, Jean-Yves is known world-wide as The Iceman. He is a living legend and one of the sport’s most sought-out instructors by both amateurs and professionals alike.

Jean-Yves also holds a black belt in Jiu-Jitsu and is actively involved in teaching the kids’ jiu-jitsu program.


Kickboxing & Jiu-Jitsu

Scott Jurgens


Sensei Scott has been studying jiu-jitsu for over 20 years and holds a 3rd degree black belt (Sandan) in Can-Ryu Jiu-Jitsu. He teaches the children’s jiu-jitsu program alongside Jean-Yves Thériault and Renshi André LeBlanc.

Scott has also been teaching the kickboxing program under the tutelage of Jean-Yves Thériault for over 15 years. He’s won numerous provincial and Canadian amateur kickboxing championships. He is also heavily involved in helping coach the next generation of young fighters realize their kickboxing goals.


Kickboxing

Steve Duperré

Steve has a black belt in karate and was a very successful competitor in the 80s and early 90s. He has been a nutrition and strength & conditioning coach for 25 years, having worked with trainers, various amateur athletes at all levels, teenagers, younger children, pregnant women and people just trying to get or stay in shape, lose weight, or improve their health. His knowledge and experience shine through his kickboxing classes.


Kickboxing

Shannon Duperré

Shannon has been a nutrition and strength & conditioning coach for more than 10 years. She has worked with women of all ages to help them lose weight, improve their health and increase their fitness conditioning to ultimately become strong and confident. Kickboxing training is an important part of her weekly regimen. She continues to be an inspiration to both men and women through her very popular high intensity kickboxing classes.
