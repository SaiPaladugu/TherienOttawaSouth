Instructors

Meet our team of dedicated instructors at Therien! Each of our coaches brings a unique set of skills and backgrounds, united by their passion for kickboxing and Jiu-Jitsu their commitment to helping students achieve their fitness and self-defense goals.

"The Iceman" Jean-Yves Theriault

Kickboxing & Jiu-Jitsu

Sensei Scott Jurgens

Scott studied Jiu Jitsu and Kickboxing for the past 20 years and holds both Sandan (3rd degree black belt) in Can Ryu Jiu Jitsu and Elite level in kickboxing. He currently teaches kickboxing under the tutelage of Jean-Yves Theriault and has been doing this for over 15 years. Previously Scott taught both adult and kids Jiu Jitsu programs for many years under the guidance of Hanshi John Therien. He's won numerous provincial and Canadian amateur kickboxing championships, and currently helps coach the next generation of young fighters realise their Kickboxing goals.


Kickboxing

Steve Duperre

Steve has a black belt in karate and was a very successful competitor in the 80s and early 90s. He has been a nutrition and strength & conditioning coach for 25 years, having worked with trainers, various amateur athletes at all levels, teenagers, younger children, pregnant women and people just trying to get or stay in shape, lose weight, or improve their health. His knowledge and experience shine through his kickboxing classes.


Kickboxing

Shannon Duperre

Shannon has been a nutrition and strength & conditioning coach for more than 10 years. She has worked with women of all ages to help them lose weight, improve their health and increase their fitness conditioning to ultimately become strong and confident. Kickboxing training is an important part of her weekly regimen. She continues to be an inspiration to both men and women through her very popular high intensity kickboxing classes.


Kickboxing

Sofia Saikaley

Sofia is our youngest Jiu Jitsu instructor. She received her Shodan 1st degree Black belt in November 2019 at the age of 15 years old. She has been training Jiu Jitsu since the age of 7 years old and has been with the Therien Jiu Jitsu and kickboxing Ottawa South team since her very first day as a white belt. Sofia is a very important member of our children’s program. Her commitment to our young students never falters. She leads our Jiu Jitsu students to success with her positive attitude and sense of focus, discipline and respect, both in and outside the dojo.
